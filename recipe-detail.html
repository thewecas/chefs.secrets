Total Time 25min
Servings 4

Masala Dosa

One of the most popular South Indian dishes, this Masala Dosa recipe is perfect for beginners. The batter for masala dosas is made with soaked lentils and rice. Then, potatoes and spices are mashed together to prepare a spicy filling for the masala dosa. This crispy and spicy Dosa recipe is often served with coconut chutney and Sambhar.

Prep Time 20 min
Total Time 30min
Servings 4

Instructions

Samosa

India's most popular snack - Samosa, Stuffed with a yummy and savoury potato filling, this aloo samosa is best enjoyed with tangy tamarind chutney and green chutney. It's a perfect tea-time snack and served as the perfect treat for small celebrations.

Servings 10

Jaljeera

Made with some easily available ingredients like mint leaves, ginger, water, coriander leaves, lemon juice, black salt, sugar, asafoetida, and tamarind; Jaljeera is one of the most popular drinks of North India. What makes it a perfect summer drink, is the use of soothing and cooling ingredients like mint and coriander leaves. If you are yearning for some tasty thirst-quencher that is different from the usual sweet drinks, then Jaljeera is a must-try.

Prep Time 2 min
Total Time 5 min
Servings 8

Instructions

Chop mint & coriander leaves Prepare the jaljeera in the blender Strain in a bowl & pour Jaljeera into glasses

Ingredients

1/2 cup mint leaves 1/2 cup coriander leaves 6 tablespoon lemon juice 2 tablespoon ginger, tamarind seedless, cumin seeds 4 tablespoon sugar 2 teaspoon black salt 8 cup water Icecube & salt as required
